What it does

A real editor that stays in step with your machine.

Not a toy. Open your actual project, change it from your phone, and watch it land on your desktop as a clean git commit.

Edit anywhere

Open any file in your synced repos with real syntax highlighting for dozens of languages — Python, JS, Go, Rust and more.

Two-way sync

Changes flow both directions. Even your desktop's unsaved edits appear on your phone within seconds — no commit required.

Run your code

Trigger a command from your phone; GitNomad runs it on your computer and streams the live output straight back to you.

Safe merges

If you and your desktop touch the same lines, you get a simple green/red review — never a wall of raw git conflict markers.

How it works

Three steps, then it just stays in sync.

1

Install the app & sign in

Grab GitNomad on Android and sign in with GitHub or Google. Every repo is private to your account.

2

Link your computer

Install the GitNomad extension in VS Code, then tap Profile → Link desktop for a one-time code. Your repos appear on the phone.

★

Edit from anywhere

Open a file, change it, save. It lands on your computer as a commit — and your desktop edits come back to your pocket. That's it.

Your code stays yours

Built to respect your work — and your privacy.

The code you sync passes through GitNomad's service only so your devices can exchange it. We don't read it, analyse it, sell it, or show ads. And you're always one tap from deleting it.

  • Your desktop's local repo is the source of truth.
  • No ads and no third-party trackers.
  • Delete your account & all cloud data from the app.
