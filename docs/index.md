## Key idea to remember

GitNomad keeps your **phone** and your **computer** in sync through a small cloud
**sync service**. On the free plan your computer does the real work, so for
syncing and running to work, your computer needs to be:

- **on**, with **VS Code open**, and
- the **GitNomad extension running and linked** to your account.

Most "it's not syncing" problems come down to the computer being offline or the
extension not running. See [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md).
